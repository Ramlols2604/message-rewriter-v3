## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional**: Enterprise-grade, trustworthy
- **Clear**: Direct, unambiguous language
- **Helpful**: Educates users about risks
- **Authoritative**: Expert in compliance

### Messaging Principles
1. **Clarity First**: No jargon unless necessary
2. **Actionable**: Always provide next steps
3. **Respectful**: Never blame users for mistakes
4. **Educational**: Explain why something is risky

## Use Cases & Positioning

### Primary Use Case
Corporate communication compliance and quality improvement

### Target Industries
1. **Healthcare**: HIPAA compliance, PHI protection
2. **Financial Services**: SEC compliance, insider trading prevention
3. **Technology/SaaS**: Data protection, customer privacy
4. **HR/Recruiting**: EEOC compliance, discrimination prevention
5. **Legal/Contracts**: Litigation management, attorney-client privilege

### Key Benefits
- **Risk Prevention**: Catch compliance issues before they become problems
- **Time Savings**: Rewrite messages instantly
- **Team Education**: Learn policy in real-time
- **Audit Protection**: Document violations and corrections
